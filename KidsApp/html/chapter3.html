Sequencing

Imagine you're telling a story. You wouldn't start with "Once upon a time, the end," right? In coding, we use sequences to tell the computer a step-by-step story. Each step is like a sentence, and together, they create a fantastic tale!

Order Matters

Just like a recipe, the order of instructions is crucial. If you bake a cake and put the icing before the batter, it might not taste as delicious! Computers are like master chefs, and they follow our recipes step by step.
