Sequencing

Imagine you're making cupcakes! Sequencing in coding is like telling the computer the steps to follow. First, we get the flour, then sugar, and mix. Next, add eggs and butter, mix again. Spoon the batter into cupcake liners, bake, and decorate with frosting. It's like giving the computer a recipe, so it knows the right order to make something special, just like our yummy cupcakes!

Order Matters

Just like a recipe, the order of instructions is crucial. If you bake a cake and put the icing before the batter, it might not taste as delicious! Computers are like master chefs, and they follow our recipes step by step.
