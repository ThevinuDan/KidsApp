What is a computer?

A computer is a machine that helps us to solve problems and do tasks in a step by step order. The computer can help you do amazing things, as long as you tell the computer exactly what you want to do. You give the computer these steps with your keyboard, mouse, and microphone, and the computer will follow the steps and answer back to you with the monitor or the speaker.

Hardware

Hardware is all the parts of the computer. Some parts you can see and touch like the screen, keyboard, and mouse. Some parts are Inside the computer like the CPU, the wires, and a tiny microphone that listens. Together, they make your computer work and do cool things, and all of these parts are called hardware.

Software

Software is a set of rules and steps for your computer. Think of it as a story that your computer follows to do amazing things. When your teacher tells you a story, you can't touch it, but you understand it, learn it, and imagine it. Like that, software is like a story of instructions, helping your computer to be a helpful friend.
