What is a computer?

A computer is like a helper that follows your instructions. You use a keyboard, mouse, and microphone to tell it what to do. The computer listens and talks to you through the screen or speaker. It's like having a special friend!

Hardware

Hardware means all the parts you can touch on a computer, like the screen, keyboard, and mouse. Some parts are hidden inside, like the CPU and wires. Together, they make the computer work and do cool things. We call all these parts "hardware."

Software

Software is like a special set of instructions for the computer. It's a bit like telling a story. Even though you can't touch the story you can read it and understand it, the computer understands and follows the instructions,To help you solve hard problems. This set of instructions is written in a special language called code.

CPU

The CPU is like the brain of the computer. Just like your brain helps you think and move, the CPU helps the computer think and control everything. It's the part that understands when you press buttons and quickly shows you things on the screen. It also reads and understands code.
